Make Mentoring Meaningful

Volarre is a service and online platform that provides measurable, scalable and impactful mentoring

Volarre Value

Protégé Driven Matching

Mentors are curated for the protégé based on profile data, peer and supervisor suggestions. The protégé selects a mentor and begins a relationship directly related to their learning need.

Automated Relationship Framework

Based on industry-leading processes, the Volarre mentoring framework makes it easy to build and sustain an impactful mentoring relationship.

Clarifying timelines, expectations, and goal-setting are all automated and faciliated with guidance at each stage of the mentoring relationship.

Measurable Impact

Visualize the impact of your mentoring program at a glance. Live analytics help you support and actively grow your mentoring program by seeing where help is needed.

Mentorship as a Service

Volarre includes a personal network manager to that will support your network's growth and impact.

Beyond a software platform, Volarre provides mentoring as a service for your organization.

Volarre Mentorship Framework

The Volarre system focuses on making mentoring fun and intuitive. We make sure that you are guided with helpful tutorials, videos and guides at each step of the mentoring process. You are prompted with questions to help kick things off and using the framework builds engagement throughout the relationship. To keep you on track - reminders are sent for key events, tasks, goals and meetings that you agree on with your mentor. Volarre takes the guess work out of mentoring and helps mentoring achieve broad organizational impact.

The Mentorship Flight

Our approach to mentoring is very much like taking a flight. There are two key people involved - the mentor (pilot) and protégé (co-pilot). They work together to get to a specific objective and usually have various stops along the way. The Volarre system guides and builds the relationship all along the way, just like air traffic control. There's also constant help and guidance available, just like a friendly steward.

Flight is fun, exciting and something we all understand. Using this metaphor conveys the complexity of mentorship in an easy and relaxed way. The Volarre system builds on this and focuses on making the mentoring relationship fun, intuitive and impactufl.
